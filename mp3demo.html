Quick Start

Try commands like: ls,cat (filename), grep (arg), fish, hello, pingpong, shell, counter, testprint

Do ALT+F1,F2,or F3 to switch terminals

Exit will quit the topmost shell in the terminal you are in

Reload the page to restart the OS
